Hello! My name is Gerard. I'm a graduate student researcher and PhD candidate in the UCLA Department of Physics and Astronomy where I work in the Particle Beam Physics Laboratory with James Rosenzweig. I'm currently working on improving the brightness of electron beams by studying the physics of material boundaries at high electric and magnetic fields as a collaborative project with the National Science Foundation Center for Bright Beams.
