Bio

I am a PhD candidate at the University of California, Los Angeles studying the complex physics at the interface between novel materials and high electric fields. I am originally from Massachusetts and began my STEM career on a FIRST robotics team and high school before transitioning to physics for my undergraduate studies at Boston University. I've worked for a number of different institutions including NASA and CERN.
